Project Requirements

Game Design Document

Game Design: what is the genre of the game? If there is, what is the story of the game? How is the gameplay of the game? How is the mechanic of the game? What is the objective of the game? What kind of experience do you expect from the player to feel when playing the game? What are the features you adopt from the other games? What are new features you add? Any design / asset sketch? System design architecture?

Project management: what has been implemented? What are to be implemented? Gantt Chart?

Video promotion: show the gameplay, the features, etc

Game

Features: What are the features implemented (please refer to the list of features described above)? How complex and creative the features implemented compared to the existing games? If there is a story to be told in the game, is the story interesting and has it successfully conveyed to the player? Are there relevant music and sound effects implemented? how complex / creative does the implementation?

Usability and Interactivity: Can the game be played immediately? Is there any instruction on how to play the game? Are the sections of the game are distinguish of each other (menu, pause, play, game over)?

Interface: How does the game look like (background, color pattern etc)?

Game Synopsis

The game is a top down shooter involving a player controlled tank battling against 20 AI enemy tanks in a bid to be the last tank standing. The game also features a seperate multiplayer environment that allows multiple players to battle it out to see who will be victorious!
